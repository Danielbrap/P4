Setter


Overview of the Position

The position called Setter is the most important position and holds the most responsibility. This is due to the fact that the setter enables all the hitters since they set the ball to a hitter. The setter's job is to call the plays for their team and are the brains of the operation since it’s their decision to where the ball goes to hit. The setter usually sets 2 to the outside 1 to the middle and 0.5 to the opposite as a ratio and a good rule of thumb. The setter is normally always the player to touch the ball second since it’s the easiest ball to set and to let the hitter hit the ball last. If the setter plays badly the whole team plays since they enable the team to work offensively and if he makes wrong decisions it always leads to points for the other team.
