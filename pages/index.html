History of Volleyball

Volleyball is a sport that originated in 1895 by a guy named William G.Morgan. He used the tennis net but raised it up, used a basketball as the ball, and the use of hands in handball the idea of sets came from innings. He initially named the sport mintonette due to similarities to badminton at the time but the actual name had no real meaning. In the year 1900, the YMCA changed the ball to be a smaller and lighter ball and renamed it to be Volley Ball (in 1952 renamed to volleyball). Then throughout the years, it was refined to be the sport we now know and love.
