Opposite

Overview of the Position

The position called Opposite is the Jack of All Trades in volleyball. Since the positions can call for everything you must be adaptable to the situation. The position is just like the outside but they’re on the right side of the court and are on opposite sides of the setter. If the setter is up the opposite is back and when the opposite is up the setter is back. The opposite position has the same jobs as an outside they hit, receive, and block but in less frequency is normally when receiving a serve both outsides and the libero do it but whenever an outside plays bad defense the opposite has to cover for him. Same thing on the attack when the outside isn’t playing well the opposite gets the ball. The special thing about the opposite is if the setter can’t get there it’s normally the opposite that gets it since they’re closer to the ball. The Opposite is the jack of all trades since they have to do everything and be adaptable for when their time comes.
