Outside


Overview of the Position

The position called Outside is considered the point machine of the team and there are usually 2 of them on a team. This position doesn’t have a special date of when it was created since it just appeared over time but its origins could be traced to 1916 in the Philippines since they created spiking. The outside has many roles on offense; it’s to make points since they’re a hitter and usually always get the most sets to hit. They are also tasked a lot defensively with blocking and when they’re in the back of the court they always receive serves and hits and have a chance to attack in the backcourt. Being outside requires being very tall to hit and block and it’s also the most taxing position due to the many roles it has.
